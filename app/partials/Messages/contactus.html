Q. What types of credit cards do you accept?
A. VISA, Mastercard, American Express and Discover

Q. What is your Return Policy?
A. We will accept merchandise returns on unused merchandise within 30 days of your purchase date. Please note that we are unable to accept returns or exchanges on embroidered garments. A full refund will be applied minus shipping and handling charges.

If your order was damaged in transit, please call Allianz customer care (855-737-7017) immediately. UPS will need to inspect the shipment and its packaging and a claim will be submitted. Items may be returned to:
Allianz Returns
Quality Resource Group
12795 16th Avenue North
Plymouth, MN 55441

Q. How can I cancel my order?
A. Please call us at (855-737-7017) to cancel your order prior to shipping. We are unable to cancel orders that have already shipped.
Q. Can I add/change/remove items to my order?
A. We cannot alter an order in any way after it has been submitted. Your order must first be cancelled and a new order submitted if changes are required.
Q. What shipping methods do you offer?
A. All shipments are sent via UPS. Shipping times are based on business days, Monday - Friday. Weekend days are not included as shipping days. We also offer Free Shipping Fridays to corporate headquarters. Explain this process
Q. How long will it take to receive my order?
A. Orders placed by 2:00 PM will ship the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM will ship the next business day. Custom printed items will ship 72 hours after order submission. UPS transit times will vary. Shipments are FOB MN.
Q. Can I expedite my shipment?
A. You may expedite your shipment in the check-out page for higher fees. Please note that time in transit is calculated with weekdays only. Saturday and Sunday are not included in shipping day calculations.
Q. Who do I contact if I have questions regarding my order.
A. Please call us at (855-737-7017) if you have questions regarding your order.
Q. What will you do with my email address?
A. We will send an order confirmation to the email address provided. We do not rent, sell, or share your email address.
Q. How long will it take for out of stock items to be restocked?
A. Each item varies as to when quantities will be restocked. Please check back daily as stock is replenished each day.
